About Me



About Me



Hi!

I'm Kristina Müller and I’m passionate about supporting people and organisations in their moment of change with the aim of living fulfilled lives radiating purpose, alignment and wellbeing.

I’m a certified coach in ‘Professional and Systemic Coaching’, with more than 8 years of business experience in developing people and professional teams.

As a certified yoga teacher Hatha-Vinyasa style, I’ve also combined coaching and yoga into an integral approach that allows people to design their own wellbeing tools to embody and integrate their change into their everyday lives.

HOW I ROLL

Respect

I believe in being truly present with my clients and hearing their stories. Your context and learning style are unique and we'll keep that top-of-mind.

Self-Empowerment

I believe our true power comes from within. Together we will discover how you can tune into your inner guide and empower yourself.

Process

I believe in coaching as a process leading you to your own answers. I'm here to create a framework that allows you to focus on reaching your goals.

Continuous Learning

I believe that learning is an ongoing journey. In that very spirit we'll be learning and discovering in a way that is both engaging and fun.

WE WILL EXPLORE

Clarity

Structure your thoughts and ideas to gain clarity on where you are now and what you want to achieve.

Awareness

Cultivate your awareness to help you understand what drives you and to take conscious steps forward.

Discovery

Discover your own potential by exploring solutions from different angles, opening up new horizons.

Stepping into Action

Experiment and try out new approaches to accomplish your goals and create the change you desire.
