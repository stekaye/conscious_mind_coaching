About Me

Hi!

I'm Kristina Müller and I’m passionate about supporting people and organisations in their moment of change with the aim of living fulfilled lives radiating alignment and wellbeing.

I’m a certified coach in ‘Professional and Systemic Coaching’, with more than 7 years of business experience in developing people and professional teams.

As a certified yoga teacher Hatha-Vinyasa style, I’ve also combined coaching and yoga into an integral approach that allows people to design their own wellbeing tools to embody and integrate their change into their everyday lives.

HOW I ROLL

Self-Empowerment

I believe in coaching as a structured process leading you to your own answers. A clear framework will enable you to focus on reaching your goals.

Continuous Learning

I believe that learning is a ongoing journey and something to be embraced. In that very spirit we will be learning and discovering together.
